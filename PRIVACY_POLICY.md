# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 28 March 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how SITimetable ("the app") handles your information.

---

## 1. Data Collection

**SITimetable does not collect any data.** The app has no backend server and nothing you enter or fetch is ever sent to us.

- **Credentials** — Your SIT username and password are entered by you, stored in the iOS Keychain on your device, and sent only to SIT's own authentication service (`fs.singaporetech.edu.sg`). We never see or receive them.
- **Timetable data** — Your class schedule and exam timetable are fetched directly from the in4SIT portal (`in4sit.singaporetech.edu.sg`) and stored locally on your device. We never see or receive this data.

---

## 2. How Your Information Is Stored

- **Credentials** are stored in the iOS Keychain, encrypted by the operating system. They are never written to disk in plain text.
- **Timetable data** is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
- **Nothing is stored on any external server.** The app has no backend.

---

## 3. Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third party. The only external communication the app performs is directly with SIT's own servers:

- `in4sit.singaporetech.edu.sg` — the official student portal
- `fs.singaporetech.edu.sg` — SIT's authentication service

---

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

- You can delete your saved credentials at any time via **Settings → Account → Sign Out**.
- You can delete all timetable data by deleting the app from your device.
- Deleting the app removes all locally stored timetable data.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

SITimetable is intended for use by Singapore Institute of Technology students. It is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
